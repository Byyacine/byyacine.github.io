About

I am a postdoctoral researcher in numerical fluid mechanics. My work focuses on data assimilation, reduced-order modelling, uncertainty quantification, and nonlinear dynamical systems, with applications to fluid flows.

This website gathers research outputs, publications, software, and selected projects.

GitHub Google Scholar ORCID LinkedIn CV

Research interests

  • Data assimilation for nonlinear and high-dimensional systems
  • Reduced-order modelling and delay-coordinate methods
  • Flow stability, transition, and turbulence
  • Scientific computing and numerical simulation
  • Uncertainty quantification and estimation from partial observations
